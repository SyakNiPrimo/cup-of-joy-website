Our Story 💛

From a dream to your daily dose of happiness — this is the Cup of Joy story, and you're part of it, Joymate!

Where It All Started

Born from Joy, Built for Community

Tucked in the heart of Rosario, La Union — right at Rosario Town Plaza, Poblacion East — Cup of Joy started as a simple idea: what if your neighborhood had a place that felt like a warm hug in a cup?

We believe great coffee and great food taste even better when shared. That's why we didn't just open a cafe — we created a community hub where Joymates gather, kwentuhan happens, and every visit feels like coming home.

From our first cup to our hundredth Joymate, everything we do is fueled by one thing: the joy of bringing people together. Dito sa Cup of Joy, hindi ka basta customer — you're family.
